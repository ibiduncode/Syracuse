COUNTERS.

Counters store and sometimes display the number of times a particular event
or process has occured often as relationship to a clock signal.
It is a circuit that has two stable state and can be used to store state information.
They are bistable storage device capable of storing one bit of a data.

REGISTERS.

One of small set of data holding places that are part of the CPU which may hold an instruction,
a storage address or any kind of data.It is used to quickly accept, store and transfer data and instructions
that are been ued immediately by CPU

FLIPFLOP.

They can lead an edge triggered, trailing edge triggered or pulse triggered.
When the clock signals to the device to make a transition from low to high and vice versa
Unlike a latch in what the output can change as soon as the input does.
Flipflop are edge sensitive while latch arelevel sensitive.
